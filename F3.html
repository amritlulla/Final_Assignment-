After you have got yourself moving, you have done the hard part. You can know start to plan a more direct route to your path to success. The first step was to get you going while also making a breif blueprint in your head so that you can think of any possible obsticles. This step is where you will have to direct most your effots into making an overall plan. If you devate from your orginal plan, dont worry because it could result in making you more successful. By the end of this stage you should have a general mindmap in order to be as efficient as possible while excuting the your time and energy accurately.

What does this mean

Again this part will be shorter or longer depending on your plan. Even if two people have the same end game getting there may be different for both parties because it is depended on each parties resources,creativity, and plan. For an example assume that 2 parties that are mutally exclusive both want to be NBA players one may be overweight with a family member in the NBA opposed to the other party who is atheltic but has no family member in the NBA. Both plans would be different because both would have to take a route in order to get to the same result, although just because one looks easier than the other, that may not be the case.
