Plan to Succeed

Now that you have taken the first few steps and have a blueprint of your sucess plan. It is time for you to plan for success. Most small business tend to have their business fail because the didnt put enough thought into this step. If you want to be successful you dont want to over or under estimate your success. This is the stage where you will have to do your extensive research to complete this stage properly.

What does this mean

This is the stage where you may want to rekindle some old relationship you had with friends or family that have the same plan for success as you. You will want to pull from there mistakes and acheivements. This is the stage where you will take from bad and good experences and try to go down a similar path to help you achieve the greatest success.
